What are Buildings?

Buildings are your properties that contain multiple rental units (houses). Think of them as apartment blocks or flat complexes.

Key Information:

  • Building Name: A unique identifier for your property
  • Address: Physical location of the building
  • Number of Houses: Maximum units this building can have
  • Occupied/Vacant: Auto-calculated from tenant assignments

Workflow:

  1. Create a building with basic details
  2. Add houses/units to the building
  3. Assign tenants to houses

Note: Buildings with occupied houses cannot be deleted.
