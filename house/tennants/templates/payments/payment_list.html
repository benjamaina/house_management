What are Payments?

Payments are records of money received from tenants. Each payment is linked to a specific rent charge and reduces the tenant's balance.

Payment Details:

  • Tenant: Who made the payment
  • Rent Charge: Which month/year the payment applies to
  • Amount: The payment amount in KES
  • Method: Cash, Mobile Money, Bank Transfer, or Cheque
  • Reference: Transaction ID (required for non-cash payments)

Partial Payments:

You can record multiple partial payments against a single rent charge. The system tracks the remaining balance automatically.

Important:

Create a rent charge first before recording payments. Payments must be linked to an existing rent charge.
