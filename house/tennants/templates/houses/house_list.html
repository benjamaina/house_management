What are Houses?

Houses are individual rental units within your buildings. Each house can be assigned to one active tenant at a time.

House Details:

  • House Number: Unique identifier within the building (e.g., A1, B2)
  • Building: Which property this house belongs to
  • Size: Description like "1 bedroom", "2 bedroom", "studio"
  • Rent Amount: Monthly rent for this unit
  • Deposit: Security deposit required

Occupancy:

  • Occupied: An active tenant is assigned
  • Vacant: Available for new tenants

Note: Only vacant houses can be assigned to new tenants.
