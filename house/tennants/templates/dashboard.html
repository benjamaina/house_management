What is this page?

The Dashboard gives you a quick overview of your entire property management portfolio at a glance.

What you'll see:

  • Stats Overview: Total buildings, houses, occupancy, and tenant count
  • Quick Actions: Fast access to add buildings, houses, tenants, and payments
  • Buildings Table: Overview of all your buildings with occupancy status
  • Recent Payments: Last 5 payments received
  • Unpaid Charges: Outstanding rent balances requiring attention

Getting Started:

  2. Add houses to that building
  3. Assign tenants to houses
  4. Create monthly rent charges
  5. Record payments as they come in
