What are Rent Charges?

Rent charges represent monthly rent obligations for each tenant. They track what tenants owe for a specific month/year.

Key Features:

  • Amount Due: Auto-filled from the tenant's house rent amount
  • Payments: Multiple payments can be made against a single charge
  • Balance: Automatically calculated (Amount Due - Total Paid)
  • Status: Shows Paid, Partial, or Unpaid based on balance

Bulk Create:

Use the "Bulk Create" button to generate rent charges for all active tenants at once for a specific month.

Send Reminders:

Use "Send Reminders" to notify tenants via SMS about upcoming or overdue rent.
