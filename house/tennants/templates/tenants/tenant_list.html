What are Tenants?

Tenants are the people who rent your houses. Each tenant is assigned to one house and receives monthly rent charges.

Tenant Information:

  • Full Name: Tenant's complete name
  • Contact: Phone number and email for communication
  • ID Number: Government-issued identification
  • House: The assigned rental unit
  • Rent Due Date: Monthly date when rent is due
  • Balance: Total outstanding rent owed

SMS Notifications:

Tenants can receive automated SMS reminders before rent is due. Enable this in the tenant's profile.

Status:

  • Active: Currently renting
  • Inactive: No longer renting (moved out)
